# Brag Plan: HTMS (Haulage Transport Management System)

## What is this app?
A Ministry of Energy (Ghana) system that turns stacks of paper haulage waybills into fuel-indexed invoices, approval-ready documents and a fully audited payment pipeline — from waybill to cedi.

## The angle
Treat a government payment system like a blockbuster. Somewhere in Ghana, electrical poles are moving across the country — and for decades the *payments* moved on paper. HTMS is the system that finally puts the trucks and the cedis on the same rails. Epic scale, real map, real documents. The drama comes from the absurd gap between "paper chase" and "one click".

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)
Dark screen. The real Ghana map draws itself in, a truck starts rolling from Tema. Slammed caps line: "HAULAGE PAYMENTS. STILL ON PAPER." — a serious statement about the world.

## Key moments (the middle)
- The Ghana regions map with the truck driving pin-to-pin (the app's actual hero visual).
- A haulage cost counting up in GHS — "FIDIC-INDEXED COSTING."
- The invoice + payment-request letter documents sliding in — "GENERATED, NOT TYPED."
- The eleven-step pipeline implied as "every action, logged."

## Outro / punchline
HTMS wordmark slams full-screen with the Ghana flag stripe and the verbatim tagline: "From waybill to cedi — without the paper chase." Music swell.

## User flow worth showing
Enter a trip → HTMS computes the fuel-indexed cost → generates the invoice + letter as PDFs. The centerpiece scenes show this: map/movement → cost count-up → documents.

## Tone
- Preset: cinematic
- Creative direction: government-logistics blockbuster; epic but real
- Interpretation: 5 wide scenes, big caps type, dramatic reveals with slow scale-ins; each declarative line lands before the next.

## Format: landscape — 1920x1080
## Duration: ~20 seconds

## Visual identity (from the project)
- Background: #141b2b (hero navy) / #0f1523 (deep section)
- Accent: #2e7d32 / #0d631b (Ministry green); Ghana flag #ce1126 red, #fcd116 gold, #006b3f green
- Text: #ffffff on dark, muted #ffffff at 70%
- Display font: heavy sans (Inter / system-ui), ALL CAPS
- Body font: system-ui / Inter
- Strongest visual element: the real Ghana regions map + truck driving pin-to-pin (Tema → Kumasi → Tamale → Yendi → Bimbilla)

## Share copy (draft)
HTMS. From waybill to cedi — without the paper chase.

## Audio direction
- Role: cinematic support that rises into a final swell
- Music: happy-beats-business-moves-vol-1 (bundled) — used as driving, purposeful bed; fade in over the hook, lift on the wordmark outro
- Music treatment: start 0s at ~0.5 vol, hold ~0.6 under the middle, swell to ~0.8 on the final logo, tail out
- Music cue guidance: read vol-1 preset strongCues; lock the wordmark reveal (Scene 2) and the final logo slam (Scene 5) to strong cues; snap the cost count-up ticks and document cards to the beat grid
- Audio-reactive treatment: subtle — the map's green glow / wordmark emphasis breathe with music RMS; no equalizers or particles
- SFX posture: sparse, motion-matched — a deep whoosh on the map draw, soft card sounds on the document reveal, a single announcement hit on the final slam
- Audio-coupled moments: cost count-up ticks; document cards arriving; final wordmark slam
- Restraint rule: no strobing, no waveform/particle clichés; text must always hold long enough to read

Map recedes/darkens. "HTMS" slams center at large scale (enters 0.95 → 1.0), Ghana flag beside it, sub: "From waybill to cedi — without the paper chase." Ministry of Energy & Green Transition · Ghana.

Audio intent: the first big lift; strong cue lands on the slam.

### Scene 3 — Fuel-indexed costing — 4s
Deep navy. Big caps "FIDIC-INDEXED COSTING." A GHS figure counts up rapidly to a total (e.g. GHS 23,697.61) — the app's real computed cost. Small caption: "surveyed distances × fuel escalation."
Sequential/interaction: yes — number counts up.
Audio intent: tense, mechanical rise resolving on the final figure.
Audio-coupled idea: count-up ticks on the beat grid.
Music: driving.
Transition mood: clean → Scene 4

### Scene 4 — Documents, generated — 4.5s
Big caps "GENERATED. NOT TYPED." Two document cards (Invoice.pdf, Payment_Request.pdf) slide/scale in with the green letterhead + table/paragraph skeletons, then a row of chips: Invoice · Letter · Memo · Signatory.

Audio intent: crisp, satisfying; soft card sounds land with each card.
Audio-coupled idea: card-by-card reveal with SFX.
Music: full.
Transition mood: soft → Scene 5

### Scene 5 — Logo slam / outro — 4s
Full-bleed. "HTMS" huge, Ghana flag stripe (red/gold/green) sweeps under it, tagline "From waybill to cedi — without the paper chase.", footer "Ministry of Energy & Green Transition · Ghana."

Audio intent: the swell + a single announcement hit; music peaks then tails.
Audio-coupled idea: beat-locked final slam, SFX ring over the tail.
Music: swell to peak, fade out.
Transition mood: dramatic hold (end)
